In this exercise, we will explore experiences of economic growth across a range of countries between 1960 and 2015. Recall that the average annual growth rate in \(Y_t\) between years \(t_1\) and \(t_0\) is given by \[\begin{equation} g = \left(\left(\frac{Y_{t_1}}{Y_{t_0}}\right)^{\frac{1}{t_1 - t_0}} - 1\right) \times 100 \end{equation}\]

# Function to calculate the growth rate
calculate_growth_rate <- function(current_value) {
  previous_value <- c(NA, current_value[-length(current_value)]) 
  growth_rate    <- ((current_value - previous_value) / previous_value) * 100
  return(growth_rate)
}
